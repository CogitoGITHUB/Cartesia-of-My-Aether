** 1. The Lattice Nodes — Candidate Compounds
*** Modafinil / Armodafinil (Provigil / Nuvigil)
:PROPERTIES:
:CLASS:    Eugeroic / Orexin-dominant
:FOCUS:    9/10
:ENERGY:   8/10
:CALM:     6-7/10 (anti-fatigue calm)
:ONSET:    1-2h
:DURATION: 12-15h
:TOLL:     Medium
:END:

- Mechanism (nerdy layer): Dopamine reuptake mild inhibition + orexin/histamine/glutamate orchestration. Promotes wakefulness without full amphetamine storm.
- Rose petals: Sustained executive function, reduced decision fatigue, cleaner cognition under load. Many report "the map becomes legible."
- Thorns & rot: Headaches, insomnia if late dosing, rare SJS, tolerance creep, subtle emotional flattening over months. Not "limitless" — subtle in well-rested high-baseline users.
- Philosophical punch: You map more nodes today. Sleep debt compounds tomorrow like unpaid cosmic tax.

*** Bupropion (Wellbutrin XL / SR)
:PROPERTIES:
:CLASS:    NDRI
:FOCUS:    7/10
:ENERGY:   8/10
:CALM:     6/10 (via mood stabilization)
:ONSET:    Days to weeks
:DURATION: Chronic baseline
:TOLL:     Medium-low
:END:

- Mechanism: Norepinephrine + dopamine reuptake inhibition. No serotonin flood.
- Rose: Motivation vector strengthens. Task initiation eases. Depressive fog lifts without heavy blunting. Weight/appetite side often welcome.
- Thorns: Seizure threshold drop (dose-dependent), initial anxiety spike, insomnia, dry mouth. Not acute — slow architecture change.
- Dark poetry: It whispers "you can move again" while the underlying decomposition continues unchanged.

*** ADHD Stimulants (Methylphenidate / Amphetamines — Concerta, Vyvanse, etc.)
:PROPERTIES:
:CLASS:    Catecholamine surge
:FOCUS:    9.5/10 (true ADHD) → 8/10 (off-label)
:ENERGY:   9/10
:CALM:     Paradoxical (noise reduction) or jitter → crash
:ONSET:    30-60min
:DURATION: 4-14h (formulation dependent)
:TOLL:     High
:END:

- Rose bloom: Laser-like 3D vectoring of attention. Internal chatter silenced. Productivity graphs spike.
- Sadistic reality: Cardiovascular load, appetite annihilation, rapid tolerance, rebound anhedonia, dependence architecture. In non-ADHD: often net loss long-term.
- Joke layer: The universe lets you borrow tomorrow's dopamine at 300% interest.

*** Calm Ancillary Nodes (when anxiety erodes the triad)
- Buspirone: Non-sedating 5-HT1A. Slow, clean, low addiction.
- Propranolol (low dose): Peripheral beta-block. Performance calm without central fog.
- Low-dose SNRIs / Vortioxetine: Baseline mood/calm scaffold, indirect focus support.
