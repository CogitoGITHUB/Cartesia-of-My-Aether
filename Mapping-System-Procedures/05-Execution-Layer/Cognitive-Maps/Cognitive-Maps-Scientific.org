* Definition
Scientific cognitive mapping studies mental representations of physical, conceptual, and social spaces, focusing on how individuals and groups encode, organize, and retrieve spatial and semantic information. It bridges cognitive psychology, geography, and information science to model internal knowledge structures.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Scientific cognitive maps align with Russian Doll structures through nested spatial/conceptual representations, where each layer adds detail to the core map. For ADHD hyperfocus, simplified cognitive maps reduce information overload, allowing hyperfocus to engage on a single map layer. Anchoring cognitive maps to specific Russian Doll layers prevents distraction by limiting visible conceptual space.
