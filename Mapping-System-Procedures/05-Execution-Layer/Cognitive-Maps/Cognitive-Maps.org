#+TITLE: Cognitive Maps
#+CATEGORY: Information Mapping Methods > ADHD-Optimized Methods

* Definition
Cognitive maps are mental representations of physical or conceptual spaces, often externalized as graphs of nodes (concepts) and edges (relationships). They help understand spatial and abstract relationships.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Concept-centric nodes align with concept thinking; maps can be layered by abstraction (outer doll = broad space, inner = details). Hyperfocus can be applied to mapping a single cognitive domain deeply.
