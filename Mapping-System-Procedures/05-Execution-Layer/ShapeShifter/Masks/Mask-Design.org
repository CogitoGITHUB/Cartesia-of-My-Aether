#+TITLE: Mask Design

* Mask Design

The ShapeShifter deploys masks — strategic projections for specific situations.

** Design Principles
- Masks are tools, not identity
- Each mask serves a purpose
- Masks are created and discarded at will

** Types of Masks
- Fear masks (command respect)
- Respect masks (build alliances)
- Obedience masks (control outcomes)

** Integration
Masks link to External-Facets mapping (People, Power, Systems).
