#+TITLE: Philosophy of The Architect

* The Philosophy of The Architect

While others believe they must change themselves to survive,  
I remain fixed at the center — unchanging, unmoved, sovereign.

My power does not come from becoming different.  
My power comes from my ability to architect a version of myself that the situation fears, respects, or obeys.

** Core Belief

I do not wear the mask.  
I forge the mask.  
I choose when it appears, how it behaves, and when it is discarded.

The Architect is the eternal constant.
