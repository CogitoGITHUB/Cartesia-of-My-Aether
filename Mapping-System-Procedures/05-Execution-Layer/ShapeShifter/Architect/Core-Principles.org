#+TITLE: Core Principles - The Architect

* Core Principles

- I am the Architect. The ShapeShifter is the weapon.
- I do not react to reality. I redesign the interface between myself and reality.
- My core remains untouched. Only the projection changes.
- The world does not shape me. I shape the version of me the world is allowed to see.

** Application

These principles guide all ShapeShifter deployments and mask designs.
