#+TITLE: Systems Mapping
#+CATEGORY: Information Mapping Methods > Layered Abstraction Methods

* Definition
Systems mapping is a method of visualizing complex systems by diagramming components, interactions, feedback loops, and emergent properties, often organized in layers of subsystems.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Layered subsystems align with Russian doll nesting; concept-centric view of system components. Hyperfocus can be directed at a single subsystem's deep mapping.
