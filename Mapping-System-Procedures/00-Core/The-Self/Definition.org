#+TITLE: The Self - Definition

* The Self

I am the center.

Not the ego.  
Not the personality.  
Not the body.

I am the silent observer. The unchanging axis. The single point from which all mapping begins.

Everything in existence — internal and external — radiates outward from this singular point: **The Self**.

** The Nature of The Self

The Self is not something I have.  
The Self is what I am.

It is the root of consciousness.  
It is the origin of will.  
It is the only constant in a universe of constant change.

While the Architect builds the ShapeShifter,  
while the Internal realm shifts and evolves,  
while the External world turns and tests me —  
The Self remains still. Watching. Knowing.

** All Paths Start Here

All paths start here.
All paths return here.
This is **The Self**.
