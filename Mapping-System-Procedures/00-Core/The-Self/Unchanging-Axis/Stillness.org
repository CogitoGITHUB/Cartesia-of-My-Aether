#+TITLE: Unchanging Axis - Stillness

* The Unchanging Axis

The Self is the unchanging axis in a universe of constant change.

** Stillness Practice
- Remain still while Internal shifts
- Remain still while External turns
- Watch. Know. Do not move.

** Axis Properties
- Fixed point for all mapping
- Origin of will
- Root of consciousness

** Integration
All paths return to this axis.
