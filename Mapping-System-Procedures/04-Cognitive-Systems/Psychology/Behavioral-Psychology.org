#+TITLE: Behavioral Psychology
#+CATEGORY: Psychology > Behavioral Psychology

* Definition
Behavioral psychology examines how conditioning, reinforcement, and learning shape behavior, informing ADHD interventions like behavioral parent training and token economies.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Informs behavioral interventions for ADHD hyperactivity/impulsivity. Russian doll layers: behaviors → reinforcement schedules → intervention protocols.
