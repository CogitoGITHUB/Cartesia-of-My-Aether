#+TITLE: Cognitive Psychology
#+CATEGORY: Psychology > Cognitive Psychology

* Definition
Cognitive psychology studies mental processes such as perception, attention, memory, language, problem-solving, and decision-making, providing frameworks for understanding ADHD executive function.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Directly studies attention, working memory, and executive function deficits in ADHD. Russian doll layers: basic processes → cognitive models → ADHD applications.
