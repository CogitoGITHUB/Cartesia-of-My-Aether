#+TITLE: ADHD Psychology
#+CATEGORY: Psychology > ADHD Psychology

* Definition
ADHD psychology studies the behavioral, emotional, and cognitive patterns of Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder, including inattention, hyperactivity, impulsivity, and associated comorbidities.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Core field for understanding ADHD hyperfocus: explains psychological mechanisms of hyperfocus, executive function deficits. Russian doll layers: symptoms → cognitive processes → psychological theories.
