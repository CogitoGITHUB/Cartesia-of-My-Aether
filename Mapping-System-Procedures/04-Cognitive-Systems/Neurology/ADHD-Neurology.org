#+TITLE: ADHD Neurology
#+CATEGORY: Neurology > ADHD Neurology

* Definition
ADHD neurology studies the brain structure, connectivity, and neurochemistry underlying Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder, focusing on cortical and subcortical differences in ADHD brains.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Directly relevant to ADHD hyperfocus: explains neurological basis of hyperfocus (dopamine, frontal-striatal connectivity). Russian doll layering can map brain regions → networks → systems.
