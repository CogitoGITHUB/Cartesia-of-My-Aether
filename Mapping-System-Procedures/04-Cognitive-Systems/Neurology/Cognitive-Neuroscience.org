#+TITLE: Cognitive Neuroscience
#+CATEGORY: Neurology > Cognitive Neuroscience

* Definition
Cognitive neuroscience examines the neural mechanisms underlying cognitive processes such as attention, memory, executive function, and language using neuroimaging and electrophysiological methods.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Central to understanding ADHD hyperfocus: studies executive function networks (frontal cortex) and dopamine systems. Russian doll layers: molecules → synapses → networks → cognition.
