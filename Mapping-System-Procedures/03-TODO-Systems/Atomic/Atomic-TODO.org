#+TITLE: Atomic TODO Definition
#+CATEGORY: ADHD Hyperfocus TODO Types > Atomic TODOs

* Definition
Atomic TODOs are granular, single-step tasks within a concept layer, representing the smallest executable unit of work in the Russian Doll hierarchy. They require no further decomposition and have clear completion criteria.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Atomic TODOs map to the innermost Russian Doll layers, providing bite-sized tasks that match short hyperfocus bursts. Their granular nature eliminates ambiguity, reducing ADHD-related task paralysis. Completing atomic TODOs in sequence builds momentum, supporting sustained hyperfocus on a single concept layer.
