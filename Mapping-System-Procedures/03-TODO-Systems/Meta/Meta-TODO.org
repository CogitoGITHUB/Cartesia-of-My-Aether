#+TITLE: Meta TODO Definition
#+CATEGORY: ADHD Hyperfocus TODO Types > Meta TODOs

* Definition
Meta TODOs are system-level tasks concerning the overall mapping system structure, including framework adjustments, hierarchy reconfigurations, and process improvements. They operate at the highest abstraction layer, governing how lower-level TODOs are structured and managed.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Meta TODOs align with the outermost Russian Doll layer, defining the boundaries and rules for all nested TODO layers. For ADHD hyperfocus, Meta TODOs are scheduled for low-distraction periods, as they require broad system thinking that can conflict with hyperfocus on granular tasks. Completing Meta TODOs first ensures the Russian Doll structure is stable before engaging hyperfocus on inner layers.
