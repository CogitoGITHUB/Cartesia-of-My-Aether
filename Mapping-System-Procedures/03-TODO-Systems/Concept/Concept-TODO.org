#+TITLE: Concept TODO Definition
#+CATEGORY: ADHD Hyperfocus TODO Types > Concept TODOs

* Definition
Concept TODOs are tasks tied to a specific concept layer in the Russian doll hierarchy, focusing on developing, linking, or refining a single concept and its immediate connections. They bridge meta-level system tasks and granular atomic tasks.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Concept TODOs map to middle Russian Doll layers, providing a focused scope for hyperfocus that is narrower than meta tasks but broader than atomic tasks. Anchoring TODOs to a single concept layer prevents task-switching distraction, allowing hyperfocus to fully engage with the concept's details and connections.
