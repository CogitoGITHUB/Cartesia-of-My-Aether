#+TITLE: Context-Anchor TODOs
#+CATEGORY: ADHD Hyperfocus TODO Types > Hyperfocus Specific

* Definition
Context-Anchor TODOs are tied to a specific concept layer and environmental/contextual cues to prevent distraction. They include explicit context markers (e.g., "Work on Concept X at Desk Y with Tool Z") to anchor hyperfocus to the target layer.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Context-Anchor TODOs lock hyperfocus to a single Russian Doll layer by pairing tasks with contextual cues that trigger and sustain focus. The context anchors act as a "locking mechanism" for hyperfocus, preventing layer-switching distraction. This aligns with ADHD's sensitivity to environmental cues, using them to reinforce focus on the target concept layer.
