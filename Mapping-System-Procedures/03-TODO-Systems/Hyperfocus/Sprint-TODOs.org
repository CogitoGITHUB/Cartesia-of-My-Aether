#+TITLE: Sprint TODOs
#+CATEGORY: ADHD Hyperfocus TODO Types > Hyperfocus Specific

* Definition
Sprint TODOs are time-boxed deep work sessions focused on a single concept layer, typically lasting 1-4 hours to match natural hyperfocus cycles. They include a predefined set of atomic or concept TODOs to be completed within the timebox.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Sprint TODOs are designed explicitly for ADHD hyperfocus cycles, time-boxing deep work to prevent hyperfocus burnout. They lock onto a single Russian Doll layer, hiding all other layers to eliminate distraction. The time boundary provides a clear end point, reducing hyperfocus overrun while maximizing productivity on the target layer.
