#+TITLE: Backburner TODOs
#+CATEGORY: ADHD Hyperfocus TODO Types > Hyperfocus Specific

* Definition
Backburner TODOs are low-priority tasks saved for periods when hyperfocus wanders or shifts between concept layers. They provide a pre-curated list of tasks to engage with when the current hyperfocus target is depleted, reducing downtime.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Backburner TODOs span multiple Russian Doll layers, allowing hyperfocus to jump to a new layer when the current one is exhausted. They prevent the "post-hyperfocus slump" by providing low-friction tasks to transition to, maintaining productivity without forcing focus on a single layer. This aligns with ADHD hyperfocus's natural tendency to shift between interests.
