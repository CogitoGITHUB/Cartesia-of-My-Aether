#+TITLE: Semantic Networks
#+CATEGORY: Information Mapping Methods > Networked Methods

* Definition
Semantic networks are knowledge representation structures where concepts are nodes and relationships are labeled edges, forming a graph that supports inference and semantic querying.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Concept nodes with relationships suit concept-centric thinking; can be layered by abstraction (more abstract concepts as outer dolls). Hyperfocus can be maintained by exploring a single node's connections in depth.
