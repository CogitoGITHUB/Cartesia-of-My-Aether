#+TITLE: Zettelkasten
#+CATEGORY: Information Mapping Methods > Networked Methods

* Definition
Zettelkasten (slip-box) is a method of personal knowledge management where notes (slips) are assigned unique IDs and linked conceptually, forming a networked, self-organizing knowledge base.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Networked concept links align with concept-centric thinking; can create nested structure by linking notes hierarchically. Hyperfocus can be maintained by following a single note thread deeply.
