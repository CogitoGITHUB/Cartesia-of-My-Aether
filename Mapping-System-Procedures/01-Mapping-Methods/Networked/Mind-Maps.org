#+TITLE: Mind Maps
#+CATEGORY: Information Mapping Methods > Networked Methods

* Definition
Mind maps are visual diagrams that organize information by connecting a central concept to related ideas via branching nodes, creating a radial network of associations.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Good for concept-centric thinking; radial structure less Russian doll-like but can adapt by focusing on one branch at a time (drill-down) to support hyperfocus. Helps externalize thoughts for ADHD executive function.
