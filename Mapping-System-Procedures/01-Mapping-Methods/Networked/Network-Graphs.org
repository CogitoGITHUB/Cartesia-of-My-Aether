#+TITLE: Network Graphs
#+CATEGORY: Information Mapping Methods > Networked Methods

* Definition
Network graphs are visual representations of entities (nodes) and their connections (edges), used to map complex relationships without hierarchical constraints.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Non-hierarchical structure may conflict with Russian doll preference; however, concept-centric nodes can be grouped into clusters (meta-dolls). Hyperfocus may be challenged by many connections; best for high-level overviews.
