#+TITLE: Grounded Theory
#+CATEGORY: Scientific Methods Info Mapping > Grounded Theory

* Definition
Grounded Theory is a qualitative research methodology that constructs theory inductively from data through systematic coding, constant comparison, and theoretical sampling.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Builds nested concepts bottom-up (inner → outer Russian doll). Concept-centric analysis aligns with hyperfocus deep dives into data.
