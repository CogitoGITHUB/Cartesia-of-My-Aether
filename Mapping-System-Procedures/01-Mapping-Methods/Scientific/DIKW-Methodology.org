#+TITLE: DIKW Methodology
#+CATEGORY: Scientific Methods & Info Mapping > DIKW Methods

* Definition
DIKW methodology applies the Data-Information-Knowledge-Wisdom hierarchy to structure research, knowledge management, and decision-making processes. It formalizes the progression from raw data to actionable wisdom through layered abstraction, aligning with systematic knowledge organization.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
The DIKW hierarchy mirrors Russian Doll nesting, with each layer (Data → Information → Knowledge → Wisdom) encapsulating the previous. For ADHD hyperfocus, DIKW provides clear layer boundaries: hyperfocus can lock onto a single layer (e.g., Knowledge) without being overwhelmed by higher or lower layers. This compartmentalization reduces distraction and supports deep work on specific abstraction levels.
