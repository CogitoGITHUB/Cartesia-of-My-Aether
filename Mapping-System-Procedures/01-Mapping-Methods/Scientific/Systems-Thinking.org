#+TITLE: Systems Thinking Methodology
#+CATEGORY: Scientific Methods Info Mapping > Systems Thinking

* Definition
Systems thinking methodology examines complex systems as wholes, focusing on interconnections, feedback loops, and emergent properties, often using causal loop diagrams and qualitative mapping.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Layered subsystems (Russian dolls) align with systems thinking. Concept-centric modeling of system components suits hyperfocus deep dives.
