#+TITLE: Alpha Taxonomy
#+CATEGORY: Scientific Methods Info Mapping > Taxonomic

* Definition
Alpha taxonomy is the discipline of identifying, describing, and naming species based on morphological characteristics, forming the foundation of biological classification.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Strict hierarchical classification (kingdom → species) matches Russian doll nesting perfectly. Concept-centric focus on taxonomic groups suits hyperfocus.
