#+TITLE: Hierarchical Concept Trees
#+CATEGORY: Information Mapping Methods > Hierarchical Methods

* Definition
Hierarchical concept trees are structured representations of knowledge where concepts are organized in a tree-like hierarchy, with broader concepts at the top and more specific sub-concepts branching downward, resembling a Russian doll nesting structure.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Perfect match for Russian doll layering: outer doll = broad concept, inner dolls = sub-concepts. Concept-centric thinking aligns with ADHD hyperfocus as you can drill down into a single branch without distraction.
