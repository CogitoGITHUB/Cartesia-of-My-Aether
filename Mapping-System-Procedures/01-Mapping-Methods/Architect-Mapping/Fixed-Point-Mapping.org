The Architect remains fixed at center — unchanging, unmoved, sovereign. This is a foundational mapping method where all design decisions radiate from a fixed point.

* The Fixed Point Principle

In system design and mathematics, a "fixed point" is a point that maps to itself under a transformation. In this system:

- The Architect is the fixed point for all design
- The Self is the fixed point for all being
- Methods, organizations, TODOs radiate from these centers

* Research on Fixed Points

** In Design Systems
Fixed points in design systems provide:
- Reference stability
- Unchanging benchmarks
- Origin for coordinate systems

** In Mathematics
A fixed point x satisfies f(x) = x. This concept from:
- Banach fixed-point theorem
- Contraction mappings
- Dynamic systems stability

Source: [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fixed_point_(mathematics)][Wikipedia - Fixed Point Mathematics]]

* Mapping Protocol

1. Identify the fixed point (Architect or Self)
2. Map outward from fixed point
3. Return to fixed point to verify
4. Use fixed point as coordinate origin
