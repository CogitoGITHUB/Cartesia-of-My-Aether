#+TITLE: Iterative Refinement Scientific Method
#+CATEGORY: Scientific Methods & Info Mapping > Iterative Refinement

* Definition
Iterative refinement applies hypothesize → test → refine cycles to develop concept structures, models, and systems incrementally. It prioritizes incremental improvement over linear progression, allowing for continuous adjustment based on feedback and new evidence.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Iterative refinement aligns with Russian Doll structures by treating each layer as a version to refine, with nested layers undergoing parallel refinement cycles. For ADHD hyperfocus, short, focused refinement cycles (mirroring sprint TODOs) match hyperfocus duration, while layer-specific refinements prevent distraction. The cyclical nature of iterative refinement supports hyperfocus by providing clear, bounded tasks per cycle.
