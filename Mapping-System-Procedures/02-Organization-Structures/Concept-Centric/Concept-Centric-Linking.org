#+TITLE: Concept-Centric Linking
#+CATEGORY: Concept Info Organization Types > Concept Centric

* Definition
Concept-centric linking organizes information around key concepts with bidirectional cross-references, creating a network of interconnected ideas rather than a linear hierarchy. It emphasizes concept relationships over positional hierarchy.

* ADHD/Hyperfocus/Russian Doll Compatibility
Concept-centric linking maps to Russian Doll layers by treating each concept as a nested unit with connections to other layers. For ADHD hyperfocus, concept anchors provide clear focus points, while cross-references are hidden until needed to prevent distraction. Hyperfocus locks onto a single concept, with links providing optional expansion to related layers without leaving the target concept.
